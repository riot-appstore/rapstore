About

RAPstore is an app store for RIOT, the friendly operating system for the Internet of Things. We want to bring developers and users of IoT apps together. As such we provide a platform to share applications for constrained IoT devices in a ready to use way.
Every 3 hours RAPstore fetches all applications which are part of examples in the RIOT master branch. App users can then select the app they need and RAPstore will provide an image for the IoT device of the users. Depending on the IoT device, installing the app either requires additional tools (e.g., flasher) or just drag and drop of the image to the device connected via USB.
In addition to apps that are part of the open RIOT repository, developers can also upload their apps to RAPstore directly.
Note that the current version is beta. We continuously work on improving RIOT and RAPstore. We highly appreciate any feedback.
